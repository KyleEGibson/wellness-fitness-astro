## Overview

Amazfit's Band 7 brings exceptional battery life and built-in Alexa convenience to budget-friendly fitness tracking, using 120 sports modes to cover a wide range of daily activity monitoring needs.

## Performance Review

Reviewers consistently praise how much less often this tracker needs charging compared to competitors, with the built-in Alexa feature getting extra praise for adding genuine voice assistant convenience directly on the wrist.

## Final Verdict

An excellent value fitness tracker for those wanting long battery life without sacrificing features — the Alexa integration and 18-day battery make it a standout budget option.
